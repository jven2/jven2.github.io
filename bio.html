John Venable Biography

I was born in November of 1996, in the town of Mequon, Wisconsin. At the age of 3, I began attending kindergarten at the University School of Milwaukee, a k-12 private institution that I would later graduate from. Throughout my time there, I joined many different clubs and sports teams, including the chess club, the soccer team, and the school boy scout troop. Upon my high school graduation, I enrolled at Tufts University, where I study Computer Science with a minor in Finance in the school of engineering.
